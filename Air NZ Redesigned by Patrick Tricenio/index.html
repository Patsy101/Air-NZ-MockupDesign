Top Things to see and do in New Zealand

Top 6 Hidden Gems

Mount Stokes Marlborough Sounds

At 1203m Mt Stokes is the highest point in the Marlborough Sounds and is an incredible spot for a different perspective of an unreal setting.

Castlepoint, Wairarapa

The Castlepoint Lighthouse is pure beauty and a jewel in the crown of the Castlepoint Scenic Reserve

Stewart Island,Southland

Stewart Island is home to one of New Zealand's Great Walks.

Gibbs Farm, Auckland

Gibbs Farm showcases an array of large-scale outdoor sculptures in the Kaipara Harbour.

Oparara Arches, West Coast

The massive limestone arch is part of the Honeycomb Hill Caves and is an easy walk suitable for all fitness levels

Motueka, Saltwater Baths

Motueka Salt Water Baths. Aim to visit high tide in the early morning or sunset,so you can practice walking on water
