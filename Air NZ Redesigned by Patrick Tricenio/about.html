Where to Next

Where to next is the start of a movement. A movement toward a sustainable approach to business. A movement of compassion & care. Air NZ is a brand on a sincere & honourable flight path to help Kiwis everywhere achieve their own ‘Where to next?’.

'Where to next' is not a literal question but a mantra to keep exploring what's possible.

Flexibility for extra peace of mind

All our NZ domestic and international fares come with flexibility options so that you can make changes to your travel plans prior to departure. You can also use your credit to book flights up to one year in advance. See when your credit expires. If you want to upgrade an existing booking to give you more flexibility, you can use your credit or Airpoints. For all options, a fare difference may apply.
